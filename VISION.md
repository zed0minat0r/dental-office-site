# Bright Smile Dental — Website Vision

## Purpose
Professional website for a small dental office that builds trust, showcases services, and makes it easy for patients to book appointments.

## Target Audience
- Local families looking for a new dentist
- Patients searching for specific dental services (cosmetic, emergency, pediatric)
- People anxious about dental visits who need reassurance
- Insurance holders looking for in-network providers

## What This Site Should Convey
- Professional, clean, and calming — not clinical or cold
- Trustworthy: real team photos (placeholder for now), credentials, patient reviews
- Modern office with latest technology
- Welcoming to all ages — families, kids, seniors
- Easy to book — appointment scheduling should be prominent
- Insurance-friendly messaging

## Design Direction
- Clean, bright, calming color palette (think soft blues, whites, warm accents)
- Friendly photography style (smiling faces, clean office spaces)
- Not overly corporate — should feel like a neighborhood practice you'd trust
- Strong CTAs for booking appointments
- Mobile-first — most patients search on their phones
