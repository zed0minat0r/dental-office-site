# Bright Smile Dental — Style Guide

## Brand Identity
- **Name**: Bright Smile Dental
- **Tagline**: Your Smile, Our Priority
- **Tone**: Warm, professional, reassuring. Never clinical or intimidating.

## Colors
- **Primary Blue**: #2B7DE9 (trust, calm, medical professionalism)
- **Secondary Teal**: #06B6D4 (fresh, clean, modern)
- **Warm Accent**: #F59E0B (friendly, approachable, CTAs)
- **Background**: #F8FAFC
- **Card Background**: #FFFFFF
- **Text Primary**: #1E293B
- **Text Secondary**: #64748B
- **Border/Divider**: #E2E8F0
- **Success Green**: #10B981
- **Gradient**: #2B7DE9 → #06B6D4

## Typography
- **Font**: Inter or system font stack
- **Headings**: 700 weight
- **Body Text**: 16px, line-height 1.6
- **Small Text**: 13-14px

## Design Principles
- Calming and welcoming — dental anxiety is real
- White space is essential — don't overcrowd
- Rounded corners (12-16px) for friendly feel
- Soft shadows, not harsh borders
- Icons should be friendly, not clinical
- Photos/illustrations of smiling people, not dental instruments
